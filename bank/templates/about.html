About this App

This demo app shows a small machine-learning workflow for predicting whether a customer will subscribe to a term deposit based on the UCI Bank Marketing dataset.

  • Form collects customer attributes used by the trained model.
  • The model returns a binary prediction and a probability if available.
  • Accuracy shown on the home page reflects the model's test accuracy (editable in app.py).

Feel free to explore the Model page for details.
